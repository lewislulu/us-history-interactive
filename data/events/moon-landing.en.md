## Background
In 1961, Kennedy announced the goal of putting a man on the moon within a decade. Through the relentless efforts of the Apollo program, 1969 finally brought the historic moment.

## Process
On July 20, the Apollo 11 lunar module "Eagle" landed in the Sea of Tranquility on the moon. Neil Armstrong became the first human to set foot on the moon.

## Result
Six hundred million people worldwide witnessed this moment on television. The moon landing became a milestone in human space exploration and a decisive victory for the United States in the Space Race.
---
### Scene: One Small Step
> Armstrong (solemnly): "That's one small step for man, one giant leap for mankind."

The astronaut slowly descended the ladder of the lunar module, his footprint marking the moon's gray dust. On Earth, hundreds of millions held their breath, then erupted in cheers.
