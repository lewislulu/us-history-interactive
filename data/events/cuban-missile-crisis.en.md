## Background
In October 1962, U.S. reconnaissance aircraft discovered Soviet nuclear missiles being deployed in Cuba, capable of striking most American cities within minutes.

## Process
Kennedy announced a naval blockade of Cuba. Both American and Soviet nuclear arsenals went to highest alert status, with the world standing at the brink of nuclear war. The standoff lasted thirteen days.

## Result
Khrushchev agreed to withdraw the missiles. The U.S. secretly promised not to invade Cuba and to withdraw missiles from Turkey. Humanity's thirteen closest days to nuclear annihilation thus ended.
